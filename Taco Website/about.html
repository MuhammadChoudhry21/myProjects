When did Tacos first appear in Canada?

Tacos first began appearing in Canada in the 1960s. The introduction of Mexican cuisine to Canadian palates can be attributed to the increased cultural exchange and travel between North America and Mexico during that period. Mexican restaurants began to emerge in Canadian cities, bringing tacos and other traditional dishes into the culinary scene. Over time, tacos have grown in popularity and have become a staple in the diverse food culture of Canada 1905.
